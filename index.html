The Story

Welcome to Tangy TD, the exciting tower defense game that puts you in charge of protecting the realm of Tangerines from waves of invading Undead summoned by an "evil" force!

In this deliciously fun game, you'll need to use your strategic skills to place your Hero Tangys along the paths and use a variety of skills and tactics to fight off the invaders and emerge victorious.

Choose from multiple thrilling missions, each with unique challenges and objectives. Level up your Tangys and invest Skillpoints into their Skilltrees. Each Skilltree offers various abilities and upgrades to permanently enhance your Tangy's power and give them an edge in combat.

Enemies come in all shapes and sizes, from nimble winged creatures to towering behemoths. Use advanced targeting and supportive buffs/abilities to create an MMO Raid-like experience. In Tangy TD, the enemies fight back!

With its unique tangy-themed world, exciting missions, and strategic gameplay, Tangy TD is a game sure to satisfy your craving for thrilling tower defense action. Don't wait - download Tangy TD today and start defending your realm!
